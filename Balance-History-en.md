# Balance History

View your account's balance adjustment history from the Finances
&amp;gt; Balance History page. For a detailed report with date ranges
and export options, build a balance history report from the Report
library.

<span id="section-id235544324698055"></span>

## View balance history

1.  In the **CertCentral** menu, go to **Finances** \> **Balance
    History**.

The **Balance History** page shows all deposits, debits, and adjustments
made to your account balance.

<span id="section-id235544326352794"></span>

## Build a balance history report

Run a balance history report to get an overview of your finances and
account balance. Reports are only stored for 90 days — download the
report locally to keep it. Building your report can take more than an
hour depending on its size. The report continues to build if you leave
the page or sign out of CertCentral. You receive an email notification
when it is available.

1.  In the **CertCentral** menu, go to **Reports** \> **Build a
    report**.

2.  Select **Balance history** from the reports category list and select
    **Next**.

3.  From the **Specify date range** dropdown, select the time frame for
    the report.

4.  In the **Choose sources** section, select the subaccounts to include
    or exclude.

5.  Select **Build report**.
