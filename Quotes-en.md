# Quotes

Generate certificate price quotes to estimate costs before purchasing.
Share quotes with stakeholders or convert them to purchase orders.

> **Notice**
>
> Generated quotes are available for 30 days before they expire.

<span id="section-id235544316845421"></span>

## Generate a quote for products

1.  In the CertCentral main menu, go to **Finances** \> **Quotes**.

2.  Select **New Quote**.

3.  In the **Quote Details** section, select the division for your
    quote. Add a description if needed..

4.  In the **Build Your Quote** section, under **Quote Type**, select
    **Products**.

5.  Enter the number of certificates, certificate type, validity period,
    and number of FQDNs or wildcard names as applicable.

6.  To add more products, select **Add Another Product**.

7.  Select **Save**.

<span id="section-id235544318426385"></span>

## Generate a quote for account credit

1.  In the CertCentral main menu, go to **Finances** \> **Quotes**.

2.  Select **New Quote**.

3.  In the **Quote Details** section, select the division for your
    quote..

4.  In the **Build Your Quote** section, select **Account Credit**.

5.  In the **Amount** box, enter the credit amount.

6.  Select **Save**.

After saving, you can download, preview, email, or convert the quote to
a purchase order.
