# Account Pricing

The Account Pricing page lists the product prices available in your
CertCentral account, including certificate types, validity periods, and
additional domain pricing.

1.  In the CertCentral main menu, go to **Finances** \> **Account
    Pricing**.

To adjust product pricing for your account or subaccounts, contact your
DigiCert account manager.
