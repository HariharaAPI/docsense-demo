# Finance Settings

Finance Settings (Finances &gt; Settings) is the page for configuring
account-level payment methods and billing contact information.

<span id="section-id235575539808398"></span>

## Configure payment methods

> **Notice**
>
> Bill to account balance must be the active payment method for
> certificates to renew automatically. Credit card payment does not
> support automatic renewal.

1.  In the CertCentral main menu, go to **Finances** \> **Settings**.

2.  Under **Payment settings** \> **Payment methods allowed**, select
    **Bill to account balance**.

3.  Under **Account Balance Notifications**, enter a threshold amount.

4.  Select **Save settings**.

CertCentral saves the payment settings. When the account balance falls
below the threshold, CertCentral sends a notification.

<span id="section-id235582042935167"></span>

## Add a value added or goods and services tax number

The Finance Settings page includes a VAT/GST identification number field
under Payment settings. CertCentral adds the number to payment records
for certificate purchases and fund deposits.

> **Important**
>
> DigiCert USA and DigiCert Japan billing entities do not support VAT or
> GST numbers. If the billing entity for your account is DigiCert, Inc
> (US) or DigiCert Japan G.K., contact your account manager before
> proceeding. DigiCert does not validate VAT or GST numbers.

1.  In the CertCentral main menu, go to **Finances** \> **Settings**.

2.  Under **Payment settings** \>, in the **VAT/GST identification
    number** field, enter your value added tax (VAT) or goods and
    services tax (GST) number.

3.  Select **Save settings**.

<span id="section-id235575543515235"></span>

## Update billing contact

1.  In the CertCentral main menu, go to **Finances** \> **Settings**.

2.  On the **Finance Settings** page, under **Billing Contact**, select
    the **Edit** link.

3.  In the **Edit Billing Contact** window, update the billing contact
    information.

4.  Select **Update Billing Contact**.

CertCentral saves the updated billing contact information.
