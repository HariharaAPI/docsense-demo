# Purchase History

Use the Purchase History page to view and download invoices for issued
certificates.

<span id="section-id235544316845421"></span>

## View an invoice

1.  In the CertCentral main menu, go to **Finances** \> **Purchase
    History**.

2.  On the **Purchase history** page, find the issued certificate.

3.  In the **View Receipt / Invoice** column, select **View Receipt /
    Invoice**.

4.  In the invoice window, view the invoice details.

<span id="section-id235544318426385"></span>

## Download an invoice

1.  In the CertCentral main menu, go to **Finances** \> **Purchase
    History**.

2.  On the **Purchase history** page, find the issued certificate.

3.  In the **Invoice** column, select **View**.

4.  On the invoice page, select **Download PDF** to download a PDF copy
    of the invoice.
