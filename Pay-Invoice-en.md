# Pay Invoice

Use the Pay Invoice page to pay outstanding invoices in your CertCentral
account.

1.  In the CertCentral main menu, go to **Finances** \> **Pay Invoice**.

2.  On the **Pay Invoice** page, fall outstanding invoices are
    preselected by default.

    Clear any invoices you do not want to pay at this time.

3.  Select your payment method.

4.  Select **Pay**.

> **Notice**
>
> If you cannot find an invoice, contact your account manager or
> DigiCert Accounts Receivable. Include your PO number and the original
> invoice number in your message.
